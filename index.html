FAQ

Do plans include 1:1 sessions?

No. The Grid is designed to be fully self‑serve. We may offer 1:1 add‑ons later.

How do Saves work?

Saves are tiny JSON files that apply settings, notes, and outcomes to the site. Paste one → the UI and copy update instantly.

What does “anti‑regression” mean?

Every change is tracked and scored. Wins are promoted; regressions are rolled back.
