FAQ

Quick answers
Can I update without coding?
Yes — paste a new index.html (like this one) and drop media into the assets folders.
What happens if media are missing?
We show a clean placeholder; nothing breaks.
Where do videos go?
assets/videos/ — e.g. gc_spin.mp4, launch_tiktok.mp4.
Where do images go?
assets/images/ — e.g. gc_logo.png, background.jpg.
