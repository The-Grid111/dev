FAQ

What does “anti-regression” mean here?

The Grid stores change sets and results. Only improvements are promoted; anything that harms outcomes is sandboxed or rolled back.

How do Saves work?

A Save is a tiny JSON containing settings and notes. Paste it into the site to sync visuals, copy, and presets instantly.

Can I use it beyond AI visuals?

Yes. The system is domain-agnostic: marketing sites, ecommerce, education, internal dashboards—anything that benefits from iterative improvement.
