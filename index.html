FAQ

What does “anti-regression” mean here?

The Grid stores change sets and results. Only improvements are promoted; anything that harms outcomes is sandboxed or rolled back.

How do Saves work?

Click Customize → Save. You get a JSON you can paste back later or share across projects.

Can I use it beyond AI visuals?

Yes. The system is domain-agnostic: content, offers, layouts, copy — anything that can be saved and measured.
