FAQ

Can I update without coding?
Yes — paste a new index.html (like this one) and drop media into the assets folders.

What happens if media are missing?
A clean placeholder shows. Nothing breaks.

Where do videos go?
assets/videos/ — e.g. gc_spin.mp4, hero_1.mp4.

Where do images go?
assets/images/ — e.g. gc_logo.png, hero_1.jpg.
